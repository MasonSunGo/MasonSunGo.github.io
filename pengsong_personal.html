Research

My research is in the area of computer graphics, with a focus on topics in computational fabrication and geometry processing. I am particularly interested in developing computational representations, methods and algorithms for designing fabricable and functional real world objects. See my Publications and YouTube channel for various objects (e.g., toys, furniture, architecture, robots) that I have designed and fabricated.

Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). Before joining SUTD in 2019, I was a research scientist in the Geometric Computing Laboratory, EPFL, working with Mark Pauly. From 2014 to 2017, I was an associate researcher in University of Science and Technology of China, working with Ligang Liu. From 2013 to 2014, I was a research fellow in Nanyang Technological University, working with Chi-Wing Fu. I received my PhD in Computer Engineering from Nanyang Technological University in 2013, under the supervision of Chi-Wing Fu. I received my master degree in 2010, and bachelor degree in 2007, both from Harbin Institute of Technology.
